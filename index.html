How to Build our Blogs (without WordPress)

  1. Commenters: use this form to write substantive comments worth including in a blog post. Substantive means either
    • Design and Code Feedback: specifics about how to improve the blog as a web page, relating to media, design, and coding.
    • Content Feedback: Share your interest in the subject by relating your thoughts and experiences to what has been written about. Ask questions! Write something the blogger will want to respond to.
  2. Bloggers:
    • Post 3+ Comments: Look through our comment responses and include any comments you consider substantive under your blog pages.
    • Address fixes from comments you agree with, and/or extend the converstaion when the comments are about the content. Paste your reply below it using the example below (inspect to see the code).
